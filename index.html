We are a group of mechanical engineering students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering Pune who aim to solve problems in a creative way. Our team consists of Ashish Kulkarni, Sarvesh Goswami and Pratik Rasne all from second year Mechanical Engineering. We have developed a rough template for the purpose of precise Agriculture. The website aims to collect the data such as moisture content, humidity and temperature with the help of IOT.It will display the current data which will be extracted from the IOT devices. It will help to save water and will help farmers on a large scale. We are still working on it and will try to devlop it more precisely over a period of time. This is just a basic template of how our website will look and the actual interface is subjected to change over a period of time with new additions.

Database

Here we will display the humidity which will be sensed by the iot device and store the humidity value. Farmers can decide wether to water or not after reading this value rather than manually checking it.

Here we will display the temperature which will be sensed by the iot device and store the temperature value. Farmers will have a clear picture looking at the temperature too. This will reduce the human effort too.

Here we will display the moisture content in the soil which will be sensed by the iot device and store the moisture value. Farmers can then decide how much to water looking at this value.
